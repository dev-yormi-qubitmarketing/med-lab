FAQ

Customer Service

Q.Do I have to make an appointment to see a pharmacist?

No. We are at your service during business hours and welcome walk-ins.

Q. Do you have medical counseling available?

Our trained medical staff is here for all prescription and medical questions. We have specialists that can assist with non-narcotic pain management, medical therapy management and prescription counseling.

Q. Do I have to call for my refills?

No, we contact you! Motto offers free text and email alerts when your next refill is available.

Q. How to I switch pharmacy’s to Motto Pharmacy?

Simply fill out the transfer prescription form or call us at 561-881-0022 or toll free at 844-684-7288 and we will handle the rest!
